The John Hodgson Legacy

John published a number of books – including, with Ernest Richards, Improvisation (published by Methuen and Co Ltd in 1966), Living Expression, also with Richards, (Ginn and Co Ltd, 1968),  Uses of Drama (Methuen and Co Ltd, 1977) and Mastering Movement: The Life and Work of Rudolf Laban (Routledge 2001).  He was researching a biography of Alan Ayckbourn when he died.
